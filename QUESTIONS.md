| 27  | Ask vs in-flight enrichment slot lock — preempt or wait? The slot lock serves the _next_ enrichment job, not the current one — so worst case the user gets Ask going while one enrichment subprocess is still finishing in the background. Acceptable because (a) enrichment usually completes within 30-60s and (b) Claude Max rate-limit can absorb a short overlap. |

## How to use

When you hit something ambiguous in the TZ that isn't a hard blocker, write a question here, make a reasonable assumption to keep moving, and surface it to the user at the next natural checkpoint.

If it _is_ a hard blocker, stop and ask before proceeding.
